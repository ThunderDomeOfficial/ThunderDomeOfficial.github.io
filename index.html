ThunderDome Home Page

The Official Website for the ThunderDome INS Server Event

Welcome to the official website of the ThunderDome! Here you will find all the info you need to understand what the ThunderDome is.
The ThunderDome is a PvP event that takes place at an arena just west of the town of Pavlovo.
You may participate in this event to earn prizes, such as:
•Rare Weapons
•Helis
•Cars
•Bricks

Not only can you get these amazing items, you can also get the satisfaction of knowing
that you proved yourself to be the best PvP'er out of 20 people!

The different gamemods in the Dome consist of:
•Team Deathmatch
•Free for All
•Capture the Flag
•1v1's

Just like any event, there are rules. These rules are fairly unrestrictive, but consist of:
•Obey and respect Dome Staff
•Do not shoot until round starts
•Do not camp outside the Dome
•Do not loot dead bodies
•Do not take anything out of supply tents

Any violations of these rules will be punished by server admins.
Yes, this event is 'Admin Sponsored'. So think twice.

If you have any questions or concerns, please send them to an Event Manager or Host in our Discord server!
